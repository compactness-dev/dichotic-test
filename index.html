Dichotic Listening Test

Different auditory stimuli played to each ear simultaneously. Identify what you heard.

Based on Kimura (1961), Shankweiler & Studdert-Kennedy (1967), and the Bergen Dichotic Listening paradigm (Hugdahl, 2003). Developed with Claude Code (Opus 4.6). Not academically validated or peer-reviewed. This is not a clinical diagnostic tool — results are for educational and exploratory purposes only.
